Asoebi Fashion: 10 Ladies Prove Orange and Pink Combo is Powerful and Classy

If you think that there are some colour combinations that are out of bounds for asoebi then you're in for a shocker as these gorgeous ladies have proven that to be false.

A Nigerian couple recently got married in Atlanta, USA, and friends of the bride made sure to show up and show out in their various asoebi styles.

Judging by photos from the event, the colours of the day were pink and orange as several ladies were pictured in the colours.

While some of the ladies opted for an all-orange look, others added some pizzazz by combining both colours.

Although one lady had Nigerians talking with her choice of outfit, the majority of the ladies came through with stunning designs.
