Some of the service offered at the dental office includes:

Dental Implants

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots that are inserted into the jawbone to replace missing teeth. Dental implants can restore the function and appearance of your smile, as well as prevent bone loss and improve your oral health.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic procedure that can lighten the color of your teeth and remove stains and discoloration. Teeth whitening can be done in-office or at home, depending on your preference and budget. Smile 360 Dental Clinic uses the latest whitening technology and products to ensure safe and effective results.

ADVERTISEMENT

Dental Braces

Dental braces are devices that are used to correct the alignment of your teeth and bite. Dental braces can improve your smile, as well as your oral health and function.

Smile 360 Dental Clinic offers various types of braces, such as metal, ceramic, lingual, and invisible braces, to suit your needs and preferences.

Enamel Veneers

Enamel veneers are thin shells of porcelain or composite resin that are bonded to the front surface of your teeth to improve their shape, size, color, and appearance. Enamel veneers can correct various cosmetic issues, such as chipped, cracked, stained, gapped, or crooked teeth.

ADVERTISEMENT

Porcelain Teeth

Porcelain teeth are artificial teeth that are made of high-quality ceramic material that mimics the natural look and feel of your teeth. Porcelain teeth can be used to replace missing or damaged teeth, or to enhance your smile. Porcelain teeth are durable, stain-resistant, and biocompatible.

Scaling and Polishing

Scaling and polishing are dental procedures that are performed to remove plaque, tartar, and stains from your teeth and gums. Scaling and polishing can prevent and treat gum disease, bad breath, and tooth decay, as well as improve the appearance of your smile.

Dental Surgery

ADVERTISEMENT

Dental surgery is a broad term that covers various procedures that involve the teeth, gums, jaw, and oral tissues. Dental surgery can be performed to treat dental infections, injuries, diseases, or defects.

Some examples of dental surgery are tooth extraction, root canal treatment, dental implant placement, gum grafting, and jaw surgery.

Dento-facial Orthopaedics

Our Dento-facial orthopaedics focuses on the growth and development of the face and jaws. Dento-facial orthopaedics can help to correct or prevent problems such as underbite, overbite, crossbite, open bite, and facial asymmetry.

Pediatric Dentistry

ADVERTISEMENT

Pediatric dentistry deals with the oral health of children from infancy to adolescence. Pediatric dentistry aims to provide preventive and therapeutic dental care for children, as well as to educate them and their parents about good oral hygiene habits.

Our Pediatric dentistry services include dental check-ups, fluoride treatments, sealants, fillings, crowns, and space maintainers.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a specialty that deals with the diagnosis, prevention, and correction of mal-positioned teeth and jaws, as well as misaligned bite patterns.

Orthodontics can improve the appearance and function of your smile, as well as your oral health and facial balance.

ADVERTISEMENT

Orthodontics can involve the use of braces, aligners, retainers, or other appliances to move the teeth and jaws into the desired position.
