How to say that you didn’t hear something well in French

In formal French:

  • Excusez-moi, je n’ai pas entendu ce que vous avez dit. (Excuse me, I didn’t hear what you just said.)

In informal French:

  • Excuse-moi, j’ai pas entendu ce que t’as dit. (Excuse me, I didn’t hear what you said.)
