Ulta Beauty is having a holiday sale that includes lipsticks under $2

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  239 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

You don't have to wait until Black Friday to shop for beauty deals.

From November 4 through November 10, Ulta Beauty is holding its Holiday Haul sale. Available in stores and online, the sale features a variety of drugstore and high-end beauty products discounted for up to 50% off.

The sale also includes bundle deals, buy-one-get-one discounts, and free gifts with certain purchases. For online shoppers, customers need to spend a minimum of $35 to get free shipping.

The least expensive products included in the sale are from Essence

The brand's entire range of lip products are featured in the Holiday Haul sale for 50% off. Pictured above is one of Essence's Ultra Last Instant Colour Lipsticks, which is discounted from $4.99 down to $2.50. The brand also has lip glosses, lip palettes, and color-changing lip products on sale.

The Holiday Haul sale features hair products, too

Flat irons can be expensive, but this sale from Ulta Beauty features one from Conair, which normally costs $44.99, discounted down to $31.49.

If you're looking for discounted hair products like shampoos and conditioners, Ulta Beauty has lowered the prices of products from brands like Ahava, Instyler, and Mad Hippie as part of the sale.
