Jake Paul vs. Mike Perry: Predictions, Odds

As for the match itself, Paul is favored to win, while it’s unlikely Perry will gain his first win as a pro boxer. This is in part to Paul’s higher knockout rate of more than 66%, compared to Perry — who is still looking for his first win as a pro.
