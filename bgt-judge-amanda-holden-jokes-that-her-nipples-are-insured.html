Britain’s Got Talent judge Amanda Holden is known for wearing some form-fitting and revealing outfits. In fact, her fashion has even been the subject of complaints from viewers. However, Holden has a sense of humor about it, joking that her nipples are “insured.”

Amanda Holden Jokes That Her Nipples Are ‘Insured’

Holden reportedly told The Sun that she has the same problem as Friends actress Jennifer Aniston when she goes braless, making her nipples more obvious under her clothes. She added of her and Aniston, “We’re very sensitive to weather conditions!”

“It doesn’t seem to matter when you see boys’ nipples, so I just don’t get why it matters when you can see girls’,” Holden pointed out. “I have no problem with it. They’ve got their own management and they’re insured!”

Holden clearly isn’t self-conscious about what she wears. In April, she shared a video of herself getting ready in a red latex dress. She even had a helper with her to pull the dress on and shine the fabric on her chest and backside.

The judge has spoken out in response to complaints about her onscreen wardrobe, saying, “Times have changed and I don’t feel anyone should feel pressure to dress for your age. It’s all about body positivity and feeling good.”

Holden Had a Wardrobe Malfunction on BGT

Holden’s wardrobe was the subject of conversation once again earlier this year while she was filming the new season of Britain’s Got Talent. The judge suffered a bit of a wardrobe malfunction while she was walking to her seat on the panel.

Holden’s microphone pack slid down her back inside her form-fitting pink dress. She joked, “Say no to crack! Literally, my mic pack has gone right down my bum. You’ll be hearing all kinds of interesting noises.”

BGT is currently airing the audition round of Series 16. Holden recently hit the Golden Buzzer for an 11-year-old singer named Olivia Lynes.
