Bayley Graham showcased his impressive tap dance skills in AGT‘s Season 17 delivering a performance that was both memorable and entertaining. Displaying his skillful tap dancing along with his engaging personality has gained him four yeses from the judges.

Who is Bayley Graham?

Bayley Graham is a young and talented tap dancer from New Zealand. In an interview, he talked about his passion and love for tap dancing. He said, “I started tap dancing when I was six,” he then went on to say that his interest in dancing came after watching the show So You Think You Can Dance.

In 2015, Bayley also won multiple titles at an event run by Garden City Tappers and Performing Arts Association Inc, called NZ Dance Awards. He posts his tap dance videos on his Instagram account quite often.

Bayley has made magic with his audition on the AGT stage. Combining both a high-energy foot-tapping performance with a personality that captures the attention of viewers. The 22-year-old entertainer has found the big break in his tap dancing career with his appearance on AGT. He has expressed his desire to make a living on what he does best, tap dancing, which he has done from an early age.

This season’s wide array of different acts has raised the bar of entertainment for the show. The variety of talents has become a lot more interesting and unique and Bayley’s tap dancing is a great addition.

His audition performance has been given praise by judges Sofia Vergara, Heidi Klum, Howie Mandel, and Simon Cowell. Mandel especially likes the positive attitude that continuously engages with the audience.

“He’s so much more than a dancer, he’s an entertainer,” Mandel said after the audition.

Will He Make it into the Finale?

Bayley’s performance at the auditions was nothing short of amazing. With this week’s lineup of other amazing talents, Bayley needs to bring something new to continually impress. Since he’s a young and bright dancer, he will surely bring an interesting new routine to the stage.

Will he be able to edge out the competition and make it to the finals? Make sure to watch America’s Got Talent on Tuesday night at 8 p.m. on NBC. Fans will find out if Bayley has made it into the finale on Wednesday night.
