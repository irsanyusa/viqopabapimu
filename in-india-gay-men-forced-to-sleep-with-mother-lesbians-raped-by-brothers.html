"The ‘corrective rapes' are carried out by the victim’s own family members in a desperate bid for ‘straight’ children.

If it is found out that she is in a relationship with another girl, elders in the family believe with the ‘would-be’, even if it’s forcibly, will cure her.

The horrific crimes are so traumatising for their victims and they often go unreported."
