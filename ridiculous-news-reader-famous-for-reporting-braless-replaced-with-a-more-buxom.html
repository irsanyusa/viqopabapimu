News reader famous for reporting braless replaced with a more buxom presenter

 Posted on June 22, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  147 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Mirror Uk reports that Enki Bracaj, who became an international celebrity by presenting a program with an open top, has been replaced by an even more buxom and braless, Greta Hoxha.

Hoxha is now the presenter reading the station’s news output, wearing the shortest of waistcoats with no bra beneath.

The 21-yr-old TV news presenter made the news all around the world with her open-top stunt at the news station in conservative city of Albania.

Some 60 percent of the city of Albania are reportedly Muslim and 20 percent Christian.

Miss Bracaj's employers at Fire TV, finally decided that she had gone too far when she agreed to do a Playboy photoshoot.

As a result, they suspended miss Bracaj, who hails from the Albanian capital Tirana, and have given the job to a replacement, who has also agreed to follow the open-top news reading tradition.
