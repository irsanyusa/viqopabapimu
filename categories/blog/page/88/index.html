What Is a Virtual Credit Card Number?

 Posted on May 27, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1099 words  |  Martina Birk

Virtual credit card numbers are digital cards available through select card issuers used in place of your actual credit card number. They hide your payment information online, creating a more secure and convenient way to make credit card purchases. Your physical card information is not shared with merchants when you use a virtual card. Sometimes, virtual credit card numbers are one-time–use numbers for a single transaction.
